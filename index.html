We Finally Meet!

Hello and thank you, for taking the time to explore through my professional portfolio. I recently decided to start listening to the little voice in the back of my head. It wasn't just a whisper, it was a calling "LEARN TO CODE!" For over 3 years I shrugged it off and stayed in my lane, I was safe: Good job, Good pay, Job security. Why risk it all?

I was living and unfulfilled life and needed to make a change. I began searching for new jobs, networking, informational interviews, chatting with friends about their jobs, research - the whole nine. And then it hit me. Follow your heart. The next day I enrolled in the Northwestern University Coding Bootcamp - and began my journey! Which has led us to this very moment in time and space. I am highly motivated to succeed and look forward to working with those to seek to push the boundaries of what is possible!
